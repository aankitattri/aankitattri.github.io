Problem Statement

Drivers stuck.
Deliveries delayed.

BK Logistics is a domestic delivery company dealing in all sizes of courier parcels. Their drivers were increasingly experiencing issues with delayed trips — impacting customer satisfaction, route efficiency, and operational costs.

The challenge was to identify the root causes spanning all three sides of the delivery ecosystem — drivers, route planners, and recipients — and design a cohesive system to address them.

Goal
Create a new process called EnRoute to improve delivery delay management — identifying root causes from driver, route planner, and recipient perspectives, and building functionalities that address each.
Approach
Mapped the full delivery user flow, identified friction points at every touchpoint, and designed a dashboard + mobile app solution integrating real-time traffic, weather data, and AI route optimisation.
Responsibilities
Research · User flow mapping · AI integration strategy · Low-fi & high-fi wireframing · Dashboard & mobile app design

Root
Causes

Delays don't stem from a single source. Three distinct stakeholders each contribute friction — understanding all three was essential to designing a complete solution.

🚚
Driver
On-Road Issues
  • Navigation issues, GPS failures
  • New to area / unfamiliar routes
  • Traffic congestion
  • Vehicle breakdown
  • Unplanned stops (food, rest)
  • Personal emergencies or accidents
🗺️
Route Planner
Planning Gaps
  • Inefficient route planning
  • Poor load distribution
  • No real-time status updates
  • Inefficient resource allocation
  • Drivers assigned to unfamiliar areas
📦
Recipient
Recipient-Side Friction
  • Incorrect address or contact info
  • Last-minute rescheduling
  • Slow verification process
  • Specific delivery time requirements
  • Special access requirements
  • Unnecessary engagement with driver
